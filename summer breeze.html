SUMMER BREEZE

Whisk yourself away to the most refreshing lemon-lime
land with this RASAM Summer Breeze recipe. It makes a
large pitcher’s worth, so it’s perfect for backyard par-
ties, barbecues, birthdays, and showers. Of course, it’s
so easy you don’t need a reason to mix it up. Any warm
day will do.

INGREDIENTS
  • 3cans frozen limeade drink
  • 2cans frozen lemonade drink
  • 2L. RASAM
  • Ice
  • 3tsp. orange extract
  • Lemon and lime, for garnish
DIRECTIONS
  • Combine all ingredients.
  • Serve over ice.
  • Garnish with lemon and lime slices.
Best served in a highball glass
Ready In:2 minutes
Serving Size:8 people
TIPS FROM RASAM

For a more colorful appearance and a fruitier flavor, add other
fruit slices as garnish, including thin-sliced strawberries, pineapple
or watermelon wedges, and fruit skewers placed in individual
glasses.
